About Casa De Nanner

Who we Are, Where we are Located, and some words about The Company.

Casa De Nanner was founded by Alan Wilson. He is the owner, proprietor, and dealer principal. He has over 15 years experience in the automotive industry. He has a technical background in automobiles and their service and repairs.

Harold is Casa De Nanner’s top salesman. He has been with us from day one, and has over 50 years experience in sales. Our customers enjoy Harold’s smiling face. Harold truly is someone you can know and trust to take care of your vehicle needs!

Casa De Nanner is located on Paper Street, in Downtown Wilmington. We are directly across the street from the world famous Paper Street Soap Company, about a mile down the road from Lou's Tavern.
